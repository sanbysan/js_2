ABOUT US

NOD is an independent Polish clothing brand. Designed especially for women who appreciate quality and comfort. Clothing NOD is a timeless classic with a modern twist, perfect outfit for every day to work or meet friends. Designs are inspired by everyday way of life, often full of rush. Our goal is to create clothes that you will not want to stop wearing and in addition will fit on many occasions. NOD's clothes are made only of Polish high-quality knitwear and are manufactured in Poland.
